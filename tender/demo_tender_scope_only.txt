PROPOSED CLASSROOM BLOCK AND DRAINAGE WORKS

General requirements and preliminaries apply.
Earthworks to reduce levels, excavate foundations, backfill and cart away surplus material.
Concrete works to strip foundations, floor slab and apron.
Finishes including plaster, paint and floor screed.
Drainage works including manholes, uPVC pipes and stormwater outfall.
External works including paving and chainlink fencing.
Contractor's equipment and plant hire may be required where indicated.
Some trench depths are to be confirmed on site before final measurement.
